How to play:

Bart’s known to be causing mayhem in the area. However today’s your lucky day, it’s the fun fair, you get 30 seconds to hit Bart.
Every time you see Bart’s face on screen click it before he moves to a new position. After 30 seconds your time will be up and a pop up will show you your total score.
